Cavalier Lacrosse

Youth

Provide players with a highly competitive club/travel team experience to prepare our players to be high performing high school lacrosse stars. Our coaching staff has D1 playing experience AND youth coaching experience.

View Our Youth Teams

High School

Prepare our players to play in college. All players will receive instruction from former college lacrosse players and current high school coaches. Our coaching staff is unrivaled, featuring former UVA standouts who truly understand the college recruiting process. Our coaches have a vast network of contacts throughout major college lacrosse programs.

View Our High School Teams

Men

Feature a collection of former college and pro all-stars combined with Cav Alunmi. Our philosophy is to provide a platform for Cav players to play with a Cav team from "cradle to grave". We have both Mens Elite (18+) and Masters (32+) teams that play in both field/outdoor and box/indoor leagues.

View Our Men's Teams

Register

Register with us today to tryout for one of our teams. Take a look at our calendar for available tryout days. After choosing a date, answer our form and we will contact you with further information.
